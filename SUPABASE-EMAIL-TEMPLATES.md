## How to Apply These Templates

### 1. Go to Supabase Dashboard
- Open your project at https://supabase.com/dashboard
- Navigate to **Authentication** → **Email Templates**

### 2. Configure Password Reset Template
- Click on **"Reset Password"** template
- Replace the default content with the Password Reset template above
- Make sure `{{ .ConfirmationURL }}` is preserved
- Click **Save**

### 3. Configure Email Confirmation Template
- Click on **"Confirm Signup"** template
- Replace the default content with the Email Confirmation template above
- Make sure `{{ .ConfirmationURL }}` is preserved
- Click **Save**

### 4. Important Notes
- **{{ .ConfirmationURL }}** is automatically generated by Supabase and includes the secure token
- The URL redirects to `/auth/reset-password` (as configured in your app)
- Links expire after 60 minutes (password reset) or 24 hours (email confirmation)
- You can customize the colors and styling to match your brand

### 5. Testing
After updating the templates:
1. Request a password reset from your app
2. Check your email - it should have the new design
3. Click the button or link
4. You should be redirected to your reset password page with a valid session

## Customization Tips

### Change Colors
- Primary Blue: `#1a4d8f`
- Secondary Blue: `#2980b9`
- Gray Text: `#7f8c8d`
- Dark Text: `#2c3e50`

### Add Your Logo
Add this before the `<h2>` tag:
```html
<div style="text-align: center; margin-bottom: 24px;">
    <img src="YOUR_LOGO_URL" alt="Godfirst Education & Tours" style="height: 60px; width: auto;">
</div>
```

Replace `YOUR_LOGO_URL` with your actual logo URL.
